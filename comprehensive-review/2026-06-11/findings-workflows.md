- [STALE] **Lines 177-178 (`bash_output_limit: 0`) — "Temporarily disabled (was 8000) to gather baseline data" is no longer accurate and should be acted on.** The comment dates to 2026-04-12 (commit 3908fad). Rewrite as a deliberate decision.

Recommend: enable compaction as safety valve (e.g. `max_context_tokens: 180000`) or implement idea A. Also line 193 "Typical model limits: ... - [STALE] Line 153: "'openhands:' is accepted as a backward-compatible alias for 'agent:'" — legacy OpenHands-origin alias with no BACKCOMPAT(remove-at-...) marker per repo convention; candidate for tagging or removal pre-1.0.
